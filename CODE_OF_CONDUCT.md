# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in the GearGuide community a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free experience for everyone.

GearGuide is an industrial monitoring platform that combines machine learning, 3D digital twins, and LLM-powered technical reasoning to predict equipment failures before they occur. We welcome contributors from all backgrounds who are interested in AI, industrial automation, and predictive maintenance.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contribute to a positive environment include:

* Treating everyone with respect and professionalism.
* Providing constructive feedback.
* Supporting contributors of all experience levels.
* Collaborating openly and sharing knowledge.
* Respecting different perspectives and ideas.
* Supporting the project and, if you find it useful, considering giving the repository a ⭐ star.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

* Harassment, discrimination, or hateful conduct.
* Personal attacks, intimidation, or trolling.
* Publishing confidential information without permission.
* Deliberately introducing malicious or insecure code.
* Any behavior that creates a hostile or unwelcoming environment.

## Responsible Development

As GearGuide focuses on industrial AI and predictive maintenance, contributors should:

* Follow secure coding practices.
* Prioritize reliability and system safety.
* Clearly document AI models and assumptions.
* Respect data privacy and confidentiality.
* Test changes thoroughly before submitting a Pull Request.

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing community standards and may take appropriate action when necessary.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including GitHub repositories, issues, pull requests, discussions, documentation, and related community channels.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported through official project communication channels.
